Oracle

The Oracle has biased the sacred knucklebones, favoring heads with a X % chance, but beware, for the quarreling gods even though they endowed you 100 they have also cursed the game with a 1-X-Y%% chance of a Black Swan event.

The divine challenge is thus:

How much of your wealth to risk and whether to back heads, favored by fate, or tails, defying the odds with a Y% chance.

Win, and you double your bet; lose, and that portion of your wealth is cast into the void. But if the Black Swan strikes, you lose half your entire fortune, no matter your bet.

Will you risk it all for glory or play it safe and squander the favor of the Oracle?

With 100 and 2 minutes, can you outwit fate, balance risk and reward, and survive the chaos to ascend to immortality?
